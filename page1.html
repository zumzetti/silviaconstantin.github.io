about me

hi there, I'm Tess, a seasoned food photographer with a passion for turning culinary moments into visual masterpieces.

with a keen eye for detail and a dedication to capturing the essence of each dish, I bring a blend of creativity and technical skill to my work. 

let me tell your culinary story through a symphony of flavors, textures, and artful composition. 

how it all started

2020

culinary awakening

a passion for both photography and gastronomy ignited during the pandemic, where the aromas of diverse cuisines and the artistry in plating captured my attention due to my long time spent on social media.

inspired by the beauty in food, i began experimenting with capturing these moments through my lens.

professional culinary collaboration

in 2022 a collaboration with a local culinary establishment presented a turning point.

working closely with chefs and witnessing their dedication, I realized the power of visual storytelling in showcasing the heart and soul of culinary creations.

this experience fueled my commitment to specializing in food photography.

2022

2023

launch into professional photography

the beginning of 2023 marked the official launch of my career as a food photographer.

armed with a fusion of technical expertise and an artist's perspective, I embarked on a journey to translate the essence of taste into visually compelling narratives.

since then, each photograph has become a testament to the love for the culinary arts and the unique stories found in every dish.
